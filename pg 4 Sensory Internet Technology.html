Sensory Internet Technology

Accessing the Internet Through Yoga Movements

Login to selected sequence (website) 1
Select your website search through positioning through mountain pose.

Scroll your Newsfeed 2 and 3
Scroll your newsfeed and select images and posts to comment upon through the vinyasa flow. Hips back into downward dog, planting forward into plank, push down into chataranga and finally point shoulders up into upward dog.

Open a private conversation 4
Push back into downward dog and point your heel into the sky in downward dog splits to access private conversations.

Control Keyboard 5
In a lunge position point arm up and use arm variations to manipulate keyboard to communicate.
