Fast and Efficient Enlightenment

Quick and Easy Steps to find enlightenment and enjoy a fully sensuous karmic experience, abandoning our urgent external climate.

  1. 1. Enter a room of 40 degrees Celcius (105 Farenheit)

  2. 2. Go through with your regular asanas for at least an hour

  3. 3. Bring water and towel, but do not leave the heat for these 60 minutes

  4. 4. Thank Bikram Choudary for his quick-fix to feeling enlightened (strenuous exercise can cause such feelings to emerge)

  5. 5. Re-enter the workplace and continue to make as much money as possible, so you can afford further hot yoga classes!

Though we practice yoga, we have never been so far removed from the Buddhist spiritual mindset in our approach towards life. Money, Power and Sex are still our prime objectives. In fact yoga classes in London are often priced between $15-$50 a class. They are therefore now a status symbol; showing how much money you have, if you have time to pay and practice; showing your power, caring about your body and health to get ahead of everyone else and be able to work harder; stripping down to bare essentials in a room packed with sweaty people doing slow and sexual movements. Comercialised industry for a commercialised world.

In the words of Bikram Chadhury Westerners... can never be spiritual!... If you can even get the body right, that much is good enough for you!
